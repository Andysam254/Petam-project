About Us

At Petam, we specialize in offering high-quality treated wooden posts designed for a variety of uses, including fencing, electrical transmission, and fiber installations. With a commitment to sustainability and durability, our products are treated to withstand harsh environmental conditions, ensuring long-lasting performance. Whether for agricultural, residential, or commercial purposes, we are your trusted partner for treated posts.

Our mission is to provide our customers with affordable, durable, and environmentally friendly treated posts while promoting the responsible use of timber products.


Our Services

  • Supply of Treated Posts: We provide a wide range of treated wooden posts, including fencing posts, fiber posts, and Kenya Power transmission poles, all customized to meet various industrial and agricultural needs.
  • Custom Treatment Solutions: We offer custom timber treatment solutions based on the specific requirements of your project to ensure optimal longevity and strength.
  • Delivery and Logistics: We manage the logistics of delivering your posts directly to your site, ensuring timely and safe delivery anywhere in the region.
